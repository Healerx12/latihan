BRIARDS

A HEAR WEAPPEN IN FUR

The briard, or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog trasitionally used as a hender and guarsian of sheep

Breed History

The briard, which is believed to have originated in France, has been conturis to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the France Army sentries ,massengers and search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing. Briard were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering. Charlemagne Napoleon, Thomas Jefferson and Laffyette all owned briard.
